About Me

I grew up in Birmingham, AL, which is also where I got a BA in English lit from Birmingham-Southern College, a small liberal arts school. After graduating, I taught ESL overseas, walked up both sides of the U.S., worked in a few restaurants, and attended grad school with a plan to become an English teacher.

Now I'm a web developer. When I'm not improving my programming chops, I'm a cyclist, guitarist, amateur baker, and parent to about a dozen houseplants.
